Executive Team
The exec team consists of the directors of all the committees of TechX. We care deeply about all the events and initiatives that we have to offer, and we strive to make our mission of empowering students through technology possible on campus.

HackMIT
HackMIT organizes MIT's largest annual undergraduate hackathon. Over the span of a weekend, a thousand hackers from all over the world come together to experiment and innovate on software and hardware projects on MIT's campus. Our team works to organize this event as well as Blueprint, a smaller-scale high school hackathon.
Directors:
Jennifer Zhang
Kimberli Zhong
xFair
xFair is a career fair and technoloby expo. It is split into two committees. The Logistics committee handles all the behind-the-scenes planning during the months leading up to xFair as well as the day of xFair. The Corporate Relations team seeks out and communicates with companies to bring their hottest new tech to xFair.
Directors:
Margaret Tian
Sina Booeshaghi
MakeMIT
MakeMIT organizes one of the world's largest hardware hackathons, bringing together hundreds of makers, designers, artists, and engineers to bring their crazy ideas to life. We need enthusiastic organizers who are passionate about fostering a creative and collaborative environment for our participating hackers!
Director:
Elizabeth Bianchini
ProjX
The ProjX team is all about enabling MIT students to build cool side-projects to display at xFair. Last year, we funded dozens of projects including a prosthetic arm, an Oculus-controlled quadcopter, “gecko” gloves, and a variety of small electric vehicles. We’re looking for students with good communication, organizational, and mentorship skills. Bonus if you enjoy building things!
Director:
Benjamin Reynolds
THINK
THINK is TechX's initiative to make science and technology research and development accessible to high school students across the nation. We organize a competition in which high schoolers submit proposals for what they would create given a $1000 budget. From all the proposals we select 4-6 finalists who are flown in to see xFair and are ultimately awarded seed funding for their projects.
Directors:
Archis Bhandarkar
Daniel Lerner
Developer Operations
With projects ranging from behind-the-scenes, mission-critical web software to public-facing marketing campaigns, DevOps allows you to contribute to TechX while developing your programming chops. Students with coding experience are encouraged to apply for a dev position!

If you're upbeat, energetic, and interested in planning events, meeting new people, and helping shape the TechX community, we'd love to have you with us!
